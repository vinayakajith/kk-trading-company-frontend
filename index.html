From Farm to Trade

Rooted in Kerala's Agricultural Legacy

For generations, our family cultivated rice paddies and spice gardens across Kerala.

In the 1960s, we began trading the very produce we grew — bringing it directly to retailers and distributors.

Today, we remain a family-managed business rooted in the same land, working with the same principles.

Rice Varieties

Palakkadan Matta Rice

Kerala's red parboiled rice. Distinct reddish grain. Available parboiled and raw.

Raw & White Rice

Milled white rice varieties. Clean grain, even texture. Supplied in bulk.

Broken Rice

Smaller grain fragments. Cost-effective, widely used in food processing and animal feed.

How We Work

No complicated supply chains. Just a clear, reliable process.

4
